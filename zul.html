I-Curro Barlow Park - Ukubhala amakhodi namarobhothi!

Uma ulapha:

  • Cishe uyazibuza ukuthi kuyini ukufaka amakhodi namarobhothi.
  • Ungase ufune ukujoyina iklabhu yokubhala ikhodi nerobhothi.
  • Ungase ube nolwazi futhi ufune ukusiza i-US ukuphatha iklabhu yethu.

    • Kuyini ukubhala ngekhodi namarobhothi?

  • Ukubhala amakhodi kuwuhlu lonke lwemiyalo namalebula, amarobhothi noma izinto zikagesi nanoma yiziphi ezinye izingxenye zobuchwepheshe bedijithali okufanele zilandelwe.
  • Amarobhothi ayizingxenye ezihlelekayo futhi ayimishini esebenza namasekhethi nezici zikagesi, ukuze enze ukuphila kwethu kube lula.
  • Kuphinde kube yisifundo esisifundiswayo ukuze siqonde ukuthi ubuchwepheshe busebenza kanjani, nokuthi busebenza kanjani. *qaphela, akukhona ukubulala amabanga abafundi.

    • Cabanga ngamarobhothi okuhlala enyangeni akhelwe ukuhlola indawo. Yaklanywa futhi yahlelwa ukuze ikwazi ukubona ukuphila, noma ezinye izimpawu zempilo yangaphandle komhlaba, futhi yahlola ngempumelelo ezinye izimo zezulu eziyingozi neziyingozi.

    Ukuze ujoyine iklabhu yethu (ama-microcourses), sicela ukhulume nezinhloko zeqembu noma othisha ababalwe ngezansi (uma ungumfundi eCurro Barlow Park):
